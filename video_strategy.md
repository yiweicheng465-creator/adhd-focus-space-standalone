# Viral Video Strategy & Script for Your ADHD App

Creating a viral video for an ADHD productivity tool requires a specific approach: high energy, relatable pain points, fast pacing, and a clear "before vs. after" transformation. The script you provided is a great starting point, but we can optimize it for platforms like TikTok, Xiaohongshu, or Instagram Reels by tightening the hook and maximizing visual engagement.

## 1. The Core Strategy

*   **The Hook (0-3 seconds):** Must immediately validate the ADHD experience. Don't ask "does anyone else feel this way?" Instead, state the feeling as a universal fact to make viewers feel seen.
*   **The Problem (3-10 seconds):** Briefly show the chaos of typical productivity apps (too many tabs, overwhelming lists, rigid structures).
*   **The Solution/Reveal (10-25 seconds):** Introduce your app as the "anti-productivity app" built specifically for how ADHD brains actually work. Highlight the AI generation aspect as a magical "cheat code."
*   **The Walkthrough (25-45 seconds):** Rapid-fire visual tour of the key features. Show, don't just tell.
*   **The Call to Action (45-60 seconds):** Clear instruction on how to get it (free trial, link in bio).

## 3. How to Make It Go Viral

*   **Pacing is Everything:** ADHD audiences have short attention spans. Cut out every single pause, "um," or "uh." The video should feel like it's moving slightly faster than normal.
*   **Visual Proof:** The screen recordings of the app must look incredibly clean and satisfying. When you show the "brain dump" feature, make it a relatable thought (e.g., "remember to buy milk").
*   **The "Secret Cheat Code" Angle:** Frame the AI generation as a hack. People love tools that do the heavy lifting for them. The idea that "AI built this for me" is highly clickable right now.
*   **Engage in the Comments:** When people comment "I need this!" or "Story of my life," reply immediately. The algorithm boosts videos with high engagement in the first hour.
*   **Use Trending Audio:** Put a trending, low-fi, or slightly upbeat instrumental track quietly in the background. It helps with algorithmic reach.
*   **Keywords/Hashtags:** Use specific tags: `#ADHD`, `#ADHDproductivity`, `#neurodivergent`, `#techhacks`, `#AItools`, `#productivityapp`.

## 4. Alternative "Storytime" Hook

If the direct approach doesn't feel right, try the "Storytime" format, which performs very well on TikTok:

*"Storytime: How I got so fed up with my ADHD paralysis that I accidentally built the ultimate productivity app using AI. I was tired of switching between Notion, my calendar, and five different timer apps..."* (Then transition into the walkthrough).
